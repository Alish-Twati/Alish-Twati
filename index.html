Discover Dabba

Dabba is more than just a cafe, it’s a culinary destination. We bring the perfect blend of vibrant flavors, comfort food, and cozy atmosphere. Whether you want a quick bite, a hearty meal, or a cup of premium coffee, Dabba is your place to enjoy it all.

Why Dabba?

At Dabba, we believe in delivering quality food with a unique twist. Our name ‘Dabba’ comes from the traditional lunchbox service that has its roots in Mumbai, where meals are delivered fresh and hot. We’ve taken this concept and elevated it to offer a wide range of delicious options that cater to every palate, using the freshest ingredients. Enjoy our signature dishes in a warm, welcoming setting.

Our specialties include hand-crafted coffees, freshly baked pastries, and a variety of dishes inspired by both local and international cuisines. From the casual coffee enthusiast to the gourmet diner, Dabba has something for everyone.
